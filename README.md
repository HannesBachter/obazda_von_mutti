# Obazda von Mama
Delicious bavarian cheese spread, best served with Brezel and Bavarian Beer (maybe with some Rettich on the side)

## Ingrediences (for 4 People)
- 1 small Onion
- 250 g Cammenbert
- 100 g Doppelfrischkäse
- 50 g soft Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika powder
- Kümmel

## Preparation
- mix everything together in a small bowl
- done :grin: enjoy
